DHRUVAM

Column 2

Dhruvam is a 1993 Indian Malayalam-language action thriller film directed by Joshiy where story and dialogue are by S. N. Swamy and A. K. Sajan and screenplay by S. N. Swamy. It stars Mammootty, Jayaram, Suresh Gopi, Vikram, Gautami and Tiger Prabhakar. The musical score and songs were composed by S. P. Venkatesh. It was Vikram's debut in Malayalam cinema


Cast ; Mammootty as Narasimha Mannadiyar ; Jayaram as Veerasimha Mannadiyar ; Gouthami as Mythili ; Rudra as Maya ; Suresh Gopi as Jose Nariman.

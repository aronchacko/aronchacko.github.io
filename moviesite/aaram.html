AARAM THAMPURAN

Column 2

Aaraam Thampuran (transl. The Sixth Lord) is a 1997 Indian Malayalam-language action drama film directed by Shaji Kailas, written by Ranjith and produced by Revathy Kalamandhir.The film completed more than 250 days theatrical run and become the highest-grossing Malayalam film of 1997. It also broke the collection records of Mohanlal-starrer Chandralekha which was released in the same year.

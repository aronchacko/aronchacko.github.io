SAMRAJYAM

Column 2

Samrajyam is a 1990 Indian Malayalam-language gangster film directed by Jomon.[1] The film stars Mammootty as Alexander, a powerful underworld don. The film narrates the story of his rise and fall. The film was a major commercial success and ran for more than 200 days in Kerala box office.[3] The film found greater success in Andhra Pradesh. The film brought by distributor for about 10 prints went on to play in 400 prints due to the huge demand in theatres.[4] The film and its protagonist Alexander has developed a cult following in the years following its release.

Vatsalyam

Column 2

Vatsalyam is a 1993 Indian Malayalam-language drama film directed by Cochin Haneefa and written by A. K. Lohithadas. The film was commercial success, and ran over 255 days in theatres. Mammootty received the 1993 Kerala State Film Award for Best Actor for his role in the film.


Cast: Mammootty as Meledathu raghavan Nair ; Geetha as Malathi (Malu) ; Sunitha as Sudha ; Siddique as Vijayakumaran Nair ; Ilavarasi as Shobha
